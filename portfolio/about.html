Gorakhpur is a city in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, on the riverine region in the Purvanchal region. It is situated 270 east of state capital Lucknow. The ancient Gorakhpur, in addition to modern, comprised the districts of Basti, Deoria, Azamgarh and parts of Nepal tarai. These region, which may be called as Gorakhpur Janpad, had been an important centre of Aryan culture and civilization.
